About Me

Welcome to my research homepage! I am Tong Gao, an Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Physics Department at Michigan Technological University. My research interests lie in the application of machine learning algorithms for biological data, physics-informed neural networks on high-dimensional data, and materials design using coarse-grained modeling.

Research Interests

  • Application of machine learning algorithms for biological data
  • Physics-informed neural networks on high-dimensional data
  • Materials design using coarse-grained modeling
